ASL Translator Overview

The Asl Translator was trained on the YOLOv8 model. We used a dataset of around 1700 images. The dataset was from Roboflow and was uploaded by David Lee, who took the photos. Approximately 80% of the data was used for training and the rest for testing and validation.
